Each year I try to attend at least one developer conference. Earlier OOPSLA was a given but it lost its appeal quite a few years back and now it is not even called OOPSLA anymore. As a die hard [Smalltalker](http://www.world.st) I instead attended the [ESUG conference](http://www.esug.org/wiki/pier/Conferences) in Brest 2009 and it was easily the most rewarding conference I ever have attended! Missed last year in Barcelona but this year I am going to [Edinburgh](http://www.esug.org/wiki/pier/Conferences/2011) for a week of Smalltalking.

I am not presenting anything but I hope I will get my [HP Touchpad](http://www.hpwebos.com/us/products/pads/touchpad/index.html) from Amazon before it starts so that I can demonstrate a [WebOS](http://www.hpwebos.com) app running on it written in [Jtalk](http://www.jtalk-project.org).

If you are going too, see you there!
